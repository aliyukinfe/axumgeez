# Code of Conduct

AxumGeez welcomes respectful collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Keep feedback specific and useful.
- Respect different backgrounds, languages, and levels of experience.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Hate speech or discriminatory language
- Publishing private information
- Spam or bad-faith disruption

## Contact

Report concerns to support@adischat.com.
